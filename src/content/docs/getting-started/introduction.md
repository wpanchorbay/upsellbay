## Purpose

UpsellBay is a premium WooCommerce-native average order value (AOV) offer engine designed to help merchants increase their sales revenue by presenting targeted, relevant offers at key moments throughout the shopper journey. Unlike full funnel builders or checkout replacements, UpsellBay operates directly within your existing checkout experience, ensuring stability and preserving merchant-designed purchase paths.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Placement Offers:** Deliver custom offers at critical touchpoints:
  - **Product Page:** Highlight add-ons or frequently bought together bundles.
  - **Cart:** Suggest cross-sells or dynamic threshold/free shipping helper offers.
  - **Checkout:** Order bumps embedded in classic checkouts and modern WooCommerce Block Checkouts.
  - **Thank-You Page:** Present follow-on second purchase options post-checkout.
- **Advanced Rules Engine:** Target offers by cart values, items, customer role, and historical purchases.
- **Aggregate Analytics:** Track views, acceptances, dismissals, and attributed AOV lift without storing shopper PII.
- **Developer-First Design:** Stable hooks, filters, and standard CRUD APIs for agencies and custom setups.

## Isolation from CartBay

UpsellBay operates independently of the CartBay plugin. It uses separate database options, namespaces, tables, and cron groups. It contains no cart-recovery email templates, sequence configurations, or recovery sequence logic.
