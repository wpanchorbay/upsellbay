## Launching Your First Offer

Follow these steps to deploy an order bump to your store's checkout page:

1. **Verify Test Mode:** Navigate to **WooCommerce > UpsellBay > Settings > General** and confirm **Test Mode** is enabled.
2. **Create Offer:** Navigate to **WooCommerce > UpsellBay > Offers** and click **Add Offer**.
3. **Configure Placements:** Set Placement to **Checkout Bump** and select an accessory product to offer.
4. **Define Pricing:** Select **Percentage Discount** and input `20` for a 20% off incentive.
5. **Publish:** Set status to **Active** and save the configuration.
6. **Test Checkout:** Visit your storefront, add a main product to your cart, proceed to checkout, and verify the offer renders above the payment options.
