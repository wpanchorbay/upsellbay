## Evaluation Events

Eligibility rules are evaluated upon these events:

- **Add to Cart:** A customer adds an item on the storefront.
- **Cart Update:** Quantity revisions or coupon applications.
- **Checkout Load:** Loading or re-evaluating checkout fields.
- **Order Processed:** Initial payment confirmation before the thank-you screen.
