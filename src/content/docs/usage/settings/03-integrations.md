## Coexistence Guidelines

Ensure stable execution when running UpsellBay with adjacent optimization extensions.

- **CartBay Integration:** Expose events or suppress checkout loops during recovered checkouts.
- **Gateway Support:** Verify compatibility switches for Stripe, WooCommerce-payments, and PayPal.
- **Conflict Notices:** Manage dismissible administrative warnings regarding detected checkout modifications.
