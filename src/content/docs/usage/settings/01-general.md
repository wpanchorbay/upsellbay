## Core Settings Settings

Configure global parameters under **WooCommerce > UpsellBay > Settings > General**.

- **Enable Plugin:** Global switch to suspend or activate storefront functions.
- **Test Mode:** Forces eligible offers to render for administrators to verify layout.
- **Uninstall Data Retention:** Toggle cleanup of database tables and options upon plugin deletion.
